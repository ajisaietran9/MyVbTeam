# MyVbTeam
A payment tracker for our team's volleyball games, and trainings! 
The website is split into 2 parts, the main dashboard, and the training dashboard.

The main dashboard displays each player's details, and outstanding balances. Those who are all up to date on their payments have a green glow around their player card.

The training dashboard displays the history of our practice sessions, the date it took place, and who has paid for which trainings. When payments are made in the training dashboard, 
this is automatically updated in the main dashboard.

Just a small personal project to help me keep track of who hasn't paid me!
